The Gavel Club of CCS is an affiliation of Toastmasters International. We empower undergraduates of all backgrounds to enhance their communication and leadership skills. Through workshops, speeches, and supportive networking, we foster growth and confidence, preparing them for future success in various endeavors. Our goal is to cultivate effective communicators and capable leaders, shaping a brighter tomorrow.
